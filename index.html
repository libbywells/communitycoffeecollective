community coffee collective

What makes our coffee and baked goods different from the rest? For starters, all of our products are fair trade, orgainic, and farmed using sustainable and where possible regenerative farming practices. Our coffee is grown in Guatemala where we have local initiatives to provide education, healthcare, and other services. We are volunteer staffed and run so that we can devote as much of what you spend as possible on those programs in Guatemala, as well as our local outreach programs. To learn more about our current local outreach programs please see our about us section.
