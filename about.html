community coffee collective

local initatives

  1. as many of our ingredients as possible are locally grown and processed, including seasonally special offerings featuring produce from roots & wisdom
  2. we donate all extra food and beverages at the end of each day to local shelters and food pantries
  3. we strongly believe that stable employment is a key part of ensuring that people with criminal backgrounds are able to reintegrate with society and transform their lives for the better, which is why we are committed to hiring people with criminal backgrounds to work in our bakery facilities
  4. all profits that are made we donate to organizations that support causes such as:
    • LGBTQ rights
    • domestic violence shelters and support for survivors
    • black lives matter and the bail project
    • animal rescue and protection
    • ending the rape kit back log and supporting survivors
    • providing mental health services for homeless and disadvantaged people
    • environmental protection and conservation
  5. all of our paid workers recieve a living wage, no matter the level of skill required by their job
  6. all staff, paid and volunteer, are encouraged to bring causes that they are passionate about to us and we will try to support them, so to learn about what specific causes your local ccc is supporting be sure to stop by in person!

guatemalan and global initiatives

  1. just like our domestic paid employees, we ensure that all of our farmers in guatemala and elsewhere are paid a living wage
  2. for every take home bag of coffee that is sold in a store we will make a donation to safe passage
  3. as well as the above listed local causes we also donate profits to support global access to clean water
